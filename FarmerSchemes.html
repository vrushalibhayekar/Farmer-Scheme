Pradhan Mantri Kisan MaanDhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

pension to small and marginal farmers after age 60.

  • Farmers (18-40 yrs) contribute a small monthly amount, matched equally by the government.
  • Spouse gets 50% of the pension upon the farmer's death.
  • Aims to provide financial stability and social security to vulnerable farmers in their old age.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be an Indian citizen.
  • Must be actively involved in agricultural activities.
  • Land ownership requirements may vary by scheme.
  • Specific schemes may target small and marginal farmers.
  • Other criteria may apply as per the specific scheme guidelines.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official government agriculture website.
  2. Identify the specific scheme you wish to apply for.
  3. Check the eligibility criteria and required documents.
  4. Fill out the application form accurately.
  5. Submit the required documents.
  6. Follow up with the concerned authorities for application status.

Important Note:

Application procedures may vary. Please refer to the official scheme guidelines for the most accurate and up-to-date information.
