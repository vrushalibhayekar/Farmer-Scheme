Problem Definition



farmer scheme aims to address key challenges faced by farmers, such as financial insecutity,limited access to technology, market volaitlity, and climate change impacts. These scheemes seek to improve farmers livelihoods by providing access to affordable credit,fair market prices, modern farming techlologies, climateresilient practices, and education
